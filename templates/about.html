About CurricuLens

Your gateway to smarter learning with AI-powered quizzes

Problem Statement

Students often struggle with retaining knowledge and identifying weak areas in their studies. Traditional learning methods lack personalized feedback, making it difficult for learners to track progress and improve efficiently.

Our Solution

CurricuLens is an interactive AI-powered quiz platform that provides a personalized learning experience. It adapts questions based on your performance, helping you focus on weak areas and track your improvement.

Features

  • Adaptive quizzes with 100 questions per session
  • Instant answer validation and detailed explanations
  • Performance tracking and analytics
  • Interactive UI with smooth transitions and animations
  • Team-driven continuous improvement and updates
